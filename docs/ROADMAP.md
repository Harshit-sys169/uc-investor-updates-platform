# Product Roadmap

## Current Status (v1.0)

✅ Multi-investor management  
✅ Draft generation with AI  
✅ Email sending and tracking  
✅ Team collaboration  
✅ Scheduling  
✅ Inbox management  
✅ Audit logging  

## Q2 2024: Analytics & Intelligence

- **Analytics Dashboard** — Engagement metrics, trends, cohort analysis
- **Advanced Email Builder** — Template library, A/B testing, send time optimization
- **Integration Expansions** — Slack, Discord, Zapier, custom webhooks

## Q3 2024: Investor Preferences

- **Preference Center** — Self-serve investor preferences
- **Segmentation** — Auto-tag investors by engagement
- **Personalization** — Customized content per investor
- **PDF Export** — Download updates as branded PDFs

## Q4 2024: Enterprise

- **Advanced Access Controls** — 2FA, SSO, API keys
- **Reporting** — Custom reports, compliance exports
- **Multi-Company Management** — Portfolio view

## 2025: Ecosystem

- **CRM Integration** — Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive
- **Workflow Automation** — Smart sends, milestone tracking
- **Industry-Specific** — SaaS, Biotech, Real Estate templates

## Long-term Vision (2026+)

- **AI Co-Pilot** — Intelligent drafting, predictive analytics
- **Investor Portal** — Updates viewing, performance tracking
- **Global Expansion** — Multi-language, regional compliance
